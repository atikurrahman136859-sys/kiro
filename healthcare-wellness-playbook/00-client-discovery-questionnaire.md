# 00 · Client Discovery Questionnaire

Goal: gather everything needed to build the strategy **before** writing a single page.
Send this to the client (or run it as a live workshop). Capture answers inline.

> Tip: Score answer completeness. Don't start strategy until the **Business**, **Goals**,
> and **Compliance** sections are fully answered — they shape everything else.
