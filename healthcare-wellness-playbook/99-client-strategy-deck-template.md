## Presenter notes (internal)
- Lead with empathy (their goal/problem), not your services.
- Spend the most time on **positioning**, **trust/compliance**, and **ROI/measurement** —
  these close healthcare clients.
- Keep slides visual; put detail in the working docs (00–07) as the appendix/backup.
- Always end on a single, clear next step.
