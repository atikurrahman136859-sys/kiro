# Healthcare & Wellness Website Strategy Playbook

A reusable, client-ready strategy framework for a 360° digital agency serving the
healthcare and wellness niche — from competitor research all the way to growth.

Use this playbook to **win clients** (show a credible, niche-specific strategy in your
pitch) and to **deliver consistently** (run every engagement with the same proven process).

---

## Who this is for

This playbook is built for an agency offering full-service ("360°") digital services to
healthcare and wellness organizations, for example:

- Clinics, private practices, dental, dermatology, physiotherapy, mental health
- Hospitals and multi-specialty groups
- Telehealth / telemedicine platforms
- Wellness brands: spas, fitness, nutrition, yoga, supplements, coaching
- Health-tech SaaS and apps
- Medical device and aesthetics providers

> Healthcare & wellness is a **high-trust, high-compliance** niche. Generic web strategies
> fail here because of HIPAA/GDPR data rules, accessibility law (ADA/WCAG), Google's
> YMYL ("Your Money or Your Life") and E-E-A-T standards, and restricted advertising
> policies. This playbook bakes those constraints into every phase.

## The end-to-end process (at a glance)

```
1. DISCOVERY        → understand the client, goals, audience, compliance scope
2. RESEARCH         → competitor audit + market & voice-of-customer
3. STRATEGY         → positioning, offer, conversion funnel, KPIs
4. BLUEPRINT        → IA/sitemap, page outlines, content plan, UX/design system
5. COMPLIANCE       → HIPAA/GDPR/WCAG/E-E-A-T baked into design & content
6. BUILD            → development, integrations, performance, technical SEO
7. MARKETING        → SEO, local, content, email, paid, social, partnerships
8. GROWTH           → analytics, CRO, retention, reporting & iteration
```

## How to customize per client (workflow)

1. **Copy the playbook folder** into a new client folder, e.g. `clients/acme-dental/`.
2. **Run discovery** using `00-client-discovery-questionnaire.md`. Fill in answers.
3. **Complete the research + strategy docs** with the client's real data.
4. **Assemble the deliverable** using `99-client-strategy-deck-template.md` — this is what
   you present to the client. Pull the highlights from the working docs into the deck.
5. **Execute** phase by phase, updating docs as living artifacts.

> Tip: Keep the working docs internal and present only the polished deck (99) to clients,
> unless a client specifically wants the full detail.

## Important note on medical & health claims

This playbook is a **marketing and web-strategy resource**, not legal or medical advice.
For any client, compliance requirements (HIPAA, GDPR, FTC health-claim rules, local
medical advertising laws, professional board rules) must be confirmed with the client's
**legal counsel and compliance officer** before launch. See `04-compliance-and-trust.md`.
