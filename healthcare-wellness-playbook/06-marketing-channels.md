# 06 · Marketing Channels (All Options)

The full channel mix for healthcare & wellness growth. Each channel notes the
**health-specific constraints** so campaigns don't get disapproved or create compliance risk.
Pull the right subset per client based on goals + budget (doc 00).

---

## A. Organic / Owned

### A1. SEO (the long-term engine)
- **On-page:** intent-matched service/condition pages, internal linking, schema.
- **Technical:** speed, mobile, crawlability, structured data (see doc 05).
- **Content:** YMYL-grade, E-E-A-T (authors + medical review + sources) — see doc 04.
- **Authority/links:** medical directories, local press, associations, guest expertise,
  digital PR. Quality > quantity (health is scrutinized).
- **Priority for clinics:** target high-intent "[service] + [city]" and "near me" queries.

### A2. Local SEO (huge for clinics/practices)
- **Google Business Profile:** complete, categorized, photos, services, Q&A, posts.
- **Reviews engine:** ethical review generation + responses (don't incentivize/fake;
  follow platform + profession rules).
- **NAP consistency** across citations/directories (Healthgrades, Zocdoc, Yelp, etc.).
- **Local pages + LocalBusiness schema**; map embeds; local link building.

### A3. Content marketing
- Blog clusters answering patient questions (symptoms, treatments, cost, prevention).
- Lead magnets: assessments, guides, checklists, webinars.
- Video: "meet the provider," procedure explainers, patient education (builds trust).
- Repurpose content across email + social.

### A4. Email & lifecycle marketing
- Capture via newsletter, guides, appointment requests (with consent).
- Flows: welcome, new-patient onboarding, appointment reminders, recall/recare,
  re-engagement, post-visit follow-up, reviews request.
- **Compliance:** consent/opt-in (CAN-SPAM/GDPR); never put PHI in marketing emails; use
  HIPAA-compliant platforms if health data is involved.

### A5. Organic social & community
- Educational, trust-building content (reels, tips, behind-the-scenes, FAQs).
- Choose channels by audience: Instagram/Facebook (wellness, local), LinkedIn (B2B/health-tech),
  YouTube (education/SEO), TikTok (wellness — strict on medical claims).
- Avoid sharing/soliciting PHI in DMs/comments; have a response protocol.

---

## B. Paid

> ⚠️ Health/wellness ads are **heavily restricted**. Plan creative + landing pages around
> each platform's policy (doc 04) to avoid disapprovals and account risk.

### B1. Google Ads
- **Search** for high-intent ("book [service] near me") — usually highest ROI for local health.
- **Performance Max / Display / YouTube** for awareness + retargeting.
- **Local Services Ads (Google Guaranteed/Screened)** for eligible providers — strong trust.
- Note: healthcare/medicines policies; limits on health-based personalized targeting;
  some categories need certification.

### B2. Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
- Great for wellness, aesthetics, awareness, lead gen, retargeting.
- Restrictions: **no targeting by health condition**; limits on before/after & "personal
  attributes" claims; sensitive-category rules; landing-page review. Keep claims general &
  positive, focus on outcomes-of-life not medical promises.

### B3. Other paid
- **TikTok / Pinterest / LinkedIn** depending on audience (LinkedIn for health-tech B2B).
- **Retargeting** site visitors (respect consent/PHI — don't retarget off PHI pages).
- **YouTube ads** for education-led demand.

---

## C. Partnerships & Other

- **Referral programs:** physician/provider referrals, partner clinics, allied health.
- **Affiliate/influencer:** vet carefully; disclose connections (FTC); avoid unsubstantiated
  health claims by creators; use medical-accurate briefs.
- **PR & media:** expert commentary, local news, health awareness days.
- **Community:** events, workshops, screenings, employer/corporate wellness, sponsorships.
- **Marketplaces/directories:** Zocdoc, Healthgrades, insurer networks, wellness marketplaces.

---

## D. Channel selection framework

Pick channels by matching **goal × timeframe × budget × audience**:

| Need | Best-fit channels |
|------|-------------------|
| Fast bookings, local | Google Search Ads, Local Services Ads, GBP/Local SEO |
| Long-term cheap traffic | SEO + content + local SEO |
| Awareness / new service | Meta/YouTube/TikTok, PR, influencers |
| Retention / repeat visits | Email lifecycle, recall flows, organic social |
| B2B / health-tech | LinkedIn (organic + ads), content, partnerships |

> Start with 2–3 channels that match the #1 goal, prove ROI, then expand. Don't spread thin.
